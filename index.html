I

TITLE

Digital Entrepreneurship: Empowering Akwa Ibom Youth with Employable High-Demand Digital Skills and Online Earnings

3
II

ABSTRACT / PROJECT SUMMARY

This proposal presents a strategic initiative by OnlineGigsServices to partner with the Akwa Ibom State Government under the ARISe Agenda to train youths in high-demand digital skills, digital entrepreneurship, personal branding, and online income systems.

The program aims to equip participants with practical, employable digital skills that enable them to earn locally and globally, including in foreign currencies. Through structured training, mentorship, and job-readiness support, beneficiaries will transition from unemployment or underemployment into skilled digital professionals and entrepreneurs.

The program will be implemented in scalable cohorts ranging from 100 to 5,000 youths, using a hybrid model of physical and virtual training, practical projects, certification, and post-training support.

4
III

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Akwa Ibom State has a large population of young people who are energetic, creative, and willing to work, yet many face:

  • Limited access to quality employment
  • Skills mismatch with modern job markets
  • Dependence on shrinking local job opportunities
  • Limited exposure to global digital work systems
  • Rising unemployment and underemployment
Traditional education alone is no longer sufficient to prepare youths for today's digital economy. There is an urgent need to equip young people with practical, income-generating digital skills that connect them to global opportunities.
IV

CONTEXT, RATIONALE & INSTITUTIONAL CAPACITY

Context

The global economy is rapidly shifting toward remote work, freelancing, digital services, and online businesses. Countries and regions that invest early in digital skills development benefit from job creation, innovation, and foreign exchange inflow.

Akwa Ibom State, through the ARISe Agenda, has shown strong commitment to youth empowerment, innovation, and economic development. This program directly supports those goals by positioning Akwa Ibom youths as competitive players in the global digital economy.

5

Rationale

Training youths in digital entrepreneurship will:

  • Reduce unemployment
  • Increase self-employment and entrepreneurship
  • Create access to foreign income
  • Improve digital inclusion
  • Strengthen the state's economic resilience

Institutional Capacity / Profile

OnlineGigsServices is a registered digital training and online entrepreneurship company focused on empowering youths with globally relevant, income-generating digital skills.

Core areas of expertise include:

  • Digital skills training
  • Freelancing and remote work systems
  • Digital entrepreneurship
  • Personal branding for global markets
  • Online income and client acquisition

The organization operates with a practical, beginner-friendly approach, combining training, mentorship, and job-readiness support.

V

GOAL

To empower Akwa Ibom youths with employable, high-demand digital skills and entrepreneurial capacity that enable sustainable income generation and participation in the global digital economy.
VI

OBJECTIVES

  • Train youths in high-demand digital skills.
  • Equip participants with digital entrepreneurship and business skills.
  • Teach personal branding for global visibility.
  • Enable participants to access online jobs, freelance work, and digital business income.
  • Increase foreign currency inflow into Akwa Ibom State.
  • Reduce youth unemployment and underemployment.

VII

METHODOLOGY

The program will adopt a practical, outcome-driven methodology:

  • Hybrid training model (physical + virtual)
  • Project-based learning
  • Real-world assignments
  • Mentorship and accountability
  • Portfolio and profile development
  • Job and income-readiness coaching

Training will be delivered through:

  • Workshops and classes
  • Digital learning platforms
  • Group coaching and mentoring
  • Hands-on projects
